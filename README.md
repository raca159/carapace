# Carapace: The Relict

Wake into a deeply simulated roguelike where immortal crustaceans breed in the trenches below, their blood-thirsty offspring infiltrate the courts above, and familiar cultists rule the ruins of a collapsed civilization. Telomerase enzyme grants immortality, and has poisoned everything it touches. Harvest it from the dead and trade in blood to survive a feudal world built on forgotten technology, where every transaction costs a piece of your humanity. A procedurally generated sandbox full of emergent stories, biological economies, and worlds that generate their own history with or without you.

---

A procedurally-generated 2D terminal RPG set in a biological grimdark world. A century after the collapse of a high-tech civilization, humanity survives in neo-medieval settlements while immortal crustacean horrors — the Great Carapace — rule from the deep Trenches. Their hybrid offspring, the Sanguine Elite, walk among humans craving blood to sustain their stolen immortality. Built with Rust, `bevy_ecs`, and a TOML-driven tag system.

Explore a decaying world — fight chitin-spawn, craft from old-world artifacts, navigate faction politics between Free Settlements and the Carapace's servants, and uncover the secrets of the telomerase enzyme that grants eternal life at a terrible cost.
