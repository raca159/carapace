THE GREAT CARAPACE

          
In the deep trenches below civilization, primordial crustaceans never stop growing. Fed on telomerase for centuries, they grow older, larger, and hungrier. Their hybrid offspring crawl upward. Their spawn walk among kings. And the deeper you descend, the closer you get to the source.

Gold is worthless. The real currency is human blood and telomerase fluid вҖ” the glowing enzyme that sustains immortals. Buy technology with vampire blood. Buy genetic modifications with your own. Every transaction costs a piece of your humanity.
